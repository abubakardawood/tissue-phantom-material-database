About

This database provides open-access mechanical characterisation data for soft silicone tissue phantoms fabricated from Ecoflex 00-10, 00-30, and 00-50 with systematically varied Silicone Thinner concentrations. The phantom elastic modulus range of 6.19–158.80 kPa encompasses the stiffness of a wide variety of healthy and pathological human soft tissues.

Each phantom was characterised through ten repeated uniaxial compression tests. Engineering and true stress–strain responses were used to fit six hyperelastic material models: Neo-Hookean, Mooney-Rivlin, Yeoh, Ogden, Veronda-Westmann, and Humphrey. Model selection is guided by the Akaike Information Criterion (AIC) and standard error (S).

The dataset supports reproducible biomechanical simulation, surgical training system development, and medical device evaluation where tissue-mimicking materials are required.
